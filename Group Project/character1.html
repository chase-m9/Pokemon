Ditto – History & Facts

History of Ditto

Ditto was first introduced in Pokémon Red and Blue in 1996. It was created to show a unique transformation mechanic—the ability to copy any Pokémon it encounters, including their moves. Over the years, Ditto has appeared in every major Pokémon generation, the anime, the trading card game, and many spinoff titles. Today, Ditto remains one of the most iconic Pokémon due to its simple design and unusual Transform ability. Its full modern data (stats, abilities, forms, sprites, etc.) is maintained and updated through sources like the PokéAPI.

Facts About Ditto

  • Base experience: 101
  • Type: Normal
  • Height: 0.3 m
  • Weight: 4.0 kg
  • Base Stats: 48 HP / 48 Attack / 48 Defense / 48 Sp. Atk / 48 Sp. Def / 48 Speed
  • Abilities: Limber (standard), Imposter (hidden)
  • Known move: Transform
  • Possible held items: Metal-Powder, Quick-Powder
  • PokéAPI ID: 132
  • No evolutions — Ditto is a standalone species
