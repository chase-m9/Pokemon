Pikachu – History & Facts

History of Pikachu

Pikachu was introduced in Pokémon Red and Blue in 1996. It was designed by Atsuko Nishida and became the mascot of the entire Pokémon franchise. Pikachu’s popularity exploded with the anime, where it became Ash Ketchum’s partner. Over time, Pikachu has appeared in every generation of Pokémon games, multiple movies, merchandise, and cross-media projects. Today, Pikachu is one of the most recognizable fictional characters in the world.

Facts about Pikachu

  • PokéAPI ID: 25
  • Type: Electric
  • Height: 0.4 m
  • Weight: 6.0 kg
  • Abilities: Static (standard), Lightning Rod (hidden)
  • Base experience: 112
  • Base Stats: 35 HP / 55 Atk / 40 Def / 50 Sp. Atk / 50 Sp. Def / 90 Speed
  • Pikachu evolves from Pichu and into Raichu
  • Pikachu stores electricity in its cheeks and releases it in attacks
  • Pikachu has multiple special forms, such as cosplay outfits and Gigantamax
