Bulbasaur – History & Facts

History of Bulbasaur

Bulbasaur was one of the original starter Pokémon introduced in Pokémon Red and Blue in 1996. Designed by Ken Sugimori, Bulbasaur is unique among starters because it begins already carrying a plant bulb on its back. As the games evolved, Bulbasaur became known for its balanced stats and dual Grass/Poison typing. It remains one of the most iconic starter Pokémon in the franchise and is featured in games, anime episodes, movies, and merchandise worldwide.

Facts About Bulbasaur

  • PokéAPI ID: 1
  • Type: Grass / Poison
  • Height: 0.7 m
  • Weight: 6.9 kg
  • Abilities: Overgrow (standard), Chlorophyll (hidden)
  • Base experience: 64
  • Base Stats: 45 HP / 49 Atk / 49 Def / 65 Sp. Atk / 65 Sp. Def / 45 Speed
  • Evolves into Ivysaur, then Venusaur
  • Bulbasaur’s bulb stores nutrients that help it grow stronger
  • It is one of the first three starter Pokémon ever created
